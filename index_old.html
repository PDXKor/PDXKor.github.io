General Statistics

Student's t-test Hypothesis testing technique used to test the mean between two samples, and whether the difference between those two means could have happened by chance. Should be used where the population is expected to be normally distributed and where the population standard deviation is not known. It is also intended to work on smaller sample sizes. For more than two samples use ANOVA.
Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) Hypothesis testing technique used to test whether there is a difference between more than two samples.
